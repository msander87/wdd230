White Space and Clean Design

A.N OTHER

www.an-other.com

On this website we can see large blank spaces that, instead of looking bad, give an impression of subtlety and aesthetics, very important attributes for a formal perfume company. Of course this is in tune with the simple, light, and small font styles.

PARC: Repetition

OCEAN PRIME

www.ocean-prime.com

This section of premises of a restaurant website seemed like a good example of repetition; we can see how each block of text is repeated over and over again, giving an impression of seriousness and professionalism. It is also a good example of proximity, alignment, and contrast.

Visual Hierarchy

Tshirtmart

www.tshirtmart.com

In this web page of a t-shirt store, the visual hierarchy is something that predominates, we can see the very large texts combined with strong colored backgrounds that make our gaze go directly to what the designer wants us to see first.
