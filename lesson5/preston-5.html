Alert ceased!

The alert established for the city for strong and persistent winds ceased, and a warning for persistent storms and rains that is in effect until tomorrow at 8 PM has been updated. According to experts, it is a cold front that affects the state, generating rains and storms, some occasionally strong. They highlighted that in stormy areas, intense rains can be registered in short periods, and occasional hail falls, intense electrical activity and strong gusts of wind.

The experts also clarified that after the strong intensity of the wind last week, this week that we started would be the last with considerable wind, since from next week there is an improvement being much less the forecast intensity, with sun at full, and with a pretty good weekend to enjoy a few days out with the family if we wanted and could.
