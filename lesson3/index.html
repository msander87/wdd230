Name: RainingOrNot

Domain: www.rainingornot.com


Purpose:

The main objective of this website is to inform the people of each community about what the weather will be like, especially for specific community events. It will also be reported daily which activities are ideal to carry out that day according to the weather. Users will also be able to see specific alerts, for what activities not to do according to the weather, or what care they should have that day according to the weather.
