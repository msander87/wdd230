Target Audience

The target audience is people from 10 to 90 years old, who are interested in planning their activities for the day, according to the weather. Young people from 10 to 18 years old want to know if the weather is appropriate for practicing certain sports or outdoor activities. Adults want to know if the weather is right for housework, outdoor work, or travel. The website is expected to be used 70% by men and 30% by women. 95% of users will see this website through cell phones.

Personas

Persona 1:

Theo, 14, is a high school student who plays soccer on his neighborhood field after school, but is always worried about the weather, because if it rains the game is canceled. He wants to have a website to inform himself which days will be the best to play soccer and that way he can plan the games with his friends.

Persona 2:

Natalia, 32, is a mother of 2 children, she works and studies, and she doesn't have much time for housework and is always aware of the weather to see if she can wash clothes. She would like to have a website where she can find out which days are the best to wash clothes.

Scenarios

Scenario 1:

Natalia is about to go to work, but she remembers that the laundry basket is full. She looks at the sky and it is very cloudy, so she looks at RainingOrNot.com and informs her that in 2 hours the sun will rise and it will not rain. Then she rejoices and puts all the dirty clothes to wash, finishes the wash and hangs them up to dry. She goes to work calmly knowing that the laundry basket is no longer full.

Scenario 2:

Theo is very worried, there are 2 hours to finish classes and his friends send him a text message saying that the day is ugly and that it will surely rain, therefore they want to cancel the soccer game. Theo enters RainingOrNot.com and sees that in 1 hour the sun will rise, he sends the link of this information to his friends, and they confirm that they will go to play soccer.

Scenario 3:

Ian loves to practice sky but he does not know if tomorrow, on his day off it will be snowing, he is wondering whether to prepare his things to go skiing, so he enters the RainingOrNot website, and sees that tomorrow it will be hotter than normal, he decides forget about the sky and plan to go to the lake tomorrow.

Scenario 4:

Hugo and Leo love to travel, they want to hike and climb mountains, they enter RainingOrNot.com, where they see that the most appropriate weather for these activities will be in 2 weeks. Therefore they make all the necessary arrangements for this little trip that will be in 2 weeks.

Scenario 5:

Julio has a lot of work to do today; he has to show several houses to different clients. He knows that the best thing about those houses are their big and beautiful patios, and that without the sun the clients will not be attracted to those beautiful patios, so early in the morning he goes to RainingOrNot.com and sees that the day will be very cloudy and with some rain. Julio decides to cancel these appointments with his clients, and takes the opportunity to call a client who is interested in an apartment on the fifth floor, which has a great view, with which he can see outside and not get bored, especially on rainy days.
