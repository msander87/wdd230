Typography

Quicksand:

This font will be used for all titles on the page. It is a fairly formal font with a slight informal style, it reflects the style of the website, which will be used by a community of people from 10 to 90+ years old, but wanting to maintain an image of professionalism.

Nunito Sans:

This font will be used for all the paragraphs, and also for the texts of the navigation bar. It contrasts very well with the font used in the titles but keeping the pattern of clarity.
